
A Component Diagram breaks down a single container into its internal building blocks—components such as services, controllers, adapters, repositories, or modules. It shows how these components collaborate to fulfill the container’s responsibilities.

---
### `Key Ideas`

- Provides a **low-level view** inside one container.
    
- Shows individual components and how they interact.
    
- Highlights responsibilities and dependency paths.
    
- Helps developers understand internal modular structure.
    
- Often used to explain backend service internals or module boundaries.
