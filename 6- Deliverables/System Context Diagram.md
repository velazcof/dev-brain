
A System Context Diagram shows your software system as a single box and illustrates how it interacts with external users, systems, and services. It defines the boundaries of the system and highlights its role within a larger environment.

---
### `Key Ideas`

- Provides the **highest-level view** of the system.
    
- Shows **actors**, **external systems**, and **interactions**.
    
- Defines what is **inside** vs **outside** the system boundary.
    
- Used to align stakeholders on scope and dependencies.
    
- Ideal for onboarding, planning, and understanding integrations.
