
The **Principle of Least Privilege (PoLP)** is a **core access-control principle** that limits users, applications, and systems to the **minimum permissions** required to perform their tasks.

---
### `Key Ideas`

- **Attack Surface Reduction:**  
    Minimizes potential damage from compromised accounts or insider threats.

- **Granular Permissions:**  
    Enforced through role-based or attribute-based access control.

- **Contextual Access:**  
    Often combined with multi-factor authentication and continuous evaluation (Zero Trust).

- **Cloud Implementation:**  
    Cloud IAM roles are scoped narrowly to specific resources or services.
