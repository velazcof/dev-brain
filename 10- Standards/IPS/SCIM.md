
**SCIM** is an **open standard** that defines how to **automate user identity management** — provisioning, updating, and deprovisioning — across systems.

---
### `Purpose`

To simplify and standardize **user lifecycle management**, reducing manual administration and ensuring consistent access control across SaaS and enterprise applications.

---
### `Structure`

- **Schema:** Defines user and group attributes (name, email, role).
    
- **Endpoints:** RESTful APIs for create, update, delete, and search operations.
    
- **Provisioning Flow:** Automates sync between IdPs and Service Providers.
