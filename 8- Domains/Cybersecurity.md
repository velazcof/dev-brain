
### `Definition`

**Cybersecurity** is the domain focused on **protecting digital systems, networks, and data** from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage.

It encompasses the strategies, processes, and technologies that ensure the **confidentiality, integrity, and availability** of information across on-premise, hybrid, and cloud environments.

---
### `Key Areas`

- **Information Security**
	Safeguarding sensitive data from unauthorized disclosure or modification.

- **Network Security**
	Protecting network infrastructure and traffic from intrusions, misuse, and disruptions.

- **Identity and Access Management (IAM)**
	Controlling and verifying who can access systems and data.

- **Data Security**
	Ensuring data protection through encryption, governance, and loss prevention techniques.

- **Threats and Vulnerabilities**
	Understanding and mitigating risks like DDoS, insider threats, and data breaches.

- **Security Frameworks**
	Standardized models and best practices (e.g., NIST Cybersecurity Framework).

---
### `Core Technologies`

- **Firewalls & Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS)**
- **Encryption & Key Management**
- **Identity Providers (IdPs) and IAM Platforms**
- **Data Loss Prevention (DLP) Solutions**
- **Endpoint Protection and Antivirus Systems**
- **Security Information and Event Management (SIEM)**
- **Vulnerability Scanners and Penetration Testing Tools**
- **Cloud Security Platforms (CSPM, CASB)**
