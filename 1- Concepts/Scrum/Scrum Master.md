
The **Scrum Master** is the **facilitator and coach** who ensures the Scrum framework is followed correctly. They help the team stay productive by removing obstacles, protecting focus, and promoting self-organization.

---
### `Key Ideas`

- **Process Expert:** Deeply understands Scrum and ensures proper application of its rules and events.
    
- **Coach & Mentor:** Guides the team in Agile practices, fostering collaboration and learning.
    
- **Impediment Remover:** Actively identifies and resolves blockers that hinder progress.
    
- **Shield & Facilitator:** Protects the team from external interference and enforces sprint timeboxes.
    
- **Non-Managerial Role:** Holds no authority over team members, ensuring trust and open communication.
