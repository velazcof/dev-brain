
Virtual machines that are **reserved for a fixed period** (typically 1–3 years) in exchange for **lower hourly rates**.

---
### `Key Ideas`

- Offer up to **60–70% cost savings** compared to on-demand pricing.
    
- Best for **steady-state or predictable workloads** that run continuously.
    
- Require an upfront or partial payment and may limit flexibility.
    
- Commitment can be **regional or instance-type specific**, depending on the provider.
